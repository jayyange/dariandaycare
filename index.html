    Academics

    Our daycare designed to meet the needs of both children and families through academic objectives below.

    Literacy

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to listen, speak, read and write as well as awareness of the sounds of language, and the relationship between letters and sounds.

    Numeracy

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to understand numbers, counting numbers, solving number problems, sorting, noticing patterns, adding and subtracting numbers.

    Art

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to learn about colors, shapes, and sizes of objects. Using paints, glue, and markers in trying out new things, and solving problems.


    Health and Environment

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to specify the part external body and its works, identify healhy foods, identifying objects contained in the environment and more.

    Relationships

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to focus on the ability to care for his/herself and others, self respect and respecting others and the ability to self-govern and more.

    Communication

    Assisting children to develop skills and ability to communicate with each other, collaborative with each other, team working and perseverance, and problem-solving skills.

    Admission

    School admission information.

    Admission Requirements

    1. Children must be between the ages of 2 and 5 years old to be eligible for admission.
    2. Children must undergo a health check-up by a pediatrician to ensure they are fit and healthy to attend the daycare
    3. Parent must fill an application form and submit to school office.
    4. A parent is required to pay Tsh 10,000 as application fee upon successful submission of the form.
    5. Parents/Guardians are responsible for paying all school fees at the correct time.

    Download application form here
    How to apply

    1. Download or obtain an application form from the Darian daycare's website or office.
    2. Fill out the application form completely and accurately, providing all required information about the child and parent/guardian
    3. Attach necessary documents to the application form.
    4. Submit the completed application form with application fee and supporting documents to the Darian daycare by the deadline specified on the form
